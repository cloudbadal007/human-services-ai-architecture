# Global Regulatory Map

This architecture maps to regulations beyond the EU AI Act:

| Regulation | Jurisdiction | Mapping |
|------------|--------------|---------|
| EU AI Act | European Union | Primary design target (Articles 9–15) |
| Colorado AI Act | Colorado, USA | Transparency, impact assessments |
| Texas AI Law | Texas, USA | Risk management, documentation |
| California | California, USA | Algorithmic accountability |
| Canada AIDA | Canada | Similar high-risk classification |

The same architectural components (audit trail, explanations, deterministic rules, human oversight) satisfy requirements across jurisdictions.
